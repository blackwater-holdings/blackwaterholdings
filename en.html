With you from 2022

Blackwater Holding Limited s.r.o. consists of a young Slovak team with technical and financial background. Our company is based on availability, precision and humanity. We work with clients on an individual basis and are not afraid of challenges or new projects. Take a look at our projects, completed or in the pipeline or contact us directly.  

Availability

We are available to clients on a daily basis, whether on the phone, by email or in person. We can discuss your needs with you, answer questions and suggest a course of action for collaboration. We can accommodate and accommodate clients on time, your time and convenience is our priority. We are available anytime, just give us a call.  

Precision

Work that is well and accurately done according to our clients' expectations is the most important part of our cooperation. We will not stop working on your project until you as a client are satisfied with it. Our team can advise, help but most importantly deliver results. If you are looking for such work, do not hesitate to get in touch.

Humanity

We treat our clients individually and with respect. Every project we do is equally valuable to us and we value every collaboration equally, we do not discriminate. You can expect us to be professional and approachable. Our clients are the foundation of our business, which is why we value them so much. If you would like to be valued and welcomed as a client, please do not hesitate to get in touch.
